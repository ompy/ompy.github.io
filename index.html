Overview

We work in applied mathematics, focusing on the numerical analysis of partial differential equations, with particular emphasis on finite element and finite difference methods. Our interests include reaction–diffusion models, higher-order numerical schemes, and, more recently, the exploration of physics-informed neural networks (PINNs) for scientific computing applications.
